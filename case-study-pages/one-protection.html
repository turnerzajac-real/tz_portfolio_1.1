The Hangar is a vibrant food and music venue featuring craft beer flights, tasty bites and live music that can serve up to 10,000 people daily. Fun Biz Conessions came to Malley to brand this new experience at the Minnesota State Fair, and then the following year wanted help bringing the experience to a new level. This time in the addition of a website that would change what it meant to be a fair vendor. The main goal was to ease the experience for fairgoers with maps, a stage list, acess to menus and also a history page to host the history of this famous airplane hangar.

Branding done by Cody Paulson, creative direction by Bryan Malley.
